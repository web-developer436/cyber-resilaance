Learn About Cybersecurity

What is Phishing?

Phishing is a fraudulent attempt to steal sensitive information via email or website.

How to Detect Phishing

Look for suspicious email addresses, unprofessional language, or unexpected attachments.

Impact of Phishing

Phishing attacks can lead to identity theft, financial loss, and security breaches.

Smishing and Vishing

Smishing is phishing via SMS, while vishing is phishing over the phone.

Stay Safe Online

Use two-factor authentication, strong passwords, and update your software regularly.

What is Pharming?

Pharming redirects you to fraudulent websites by manipulating your DNS or browser settings.
