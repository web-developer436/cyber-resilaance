Learn About Cybersecurity

What is Phishing?

Phishing is a fraudulent attempt to steal sensitive information via email or website. It tricks users into revealing their passwords and personal information.

How to Detect Phishing

Look for suspicious email addresses, unprofessional language, or unexpected attachments. Check the URL to ensure it's legitimate.

Impact of Phishing

Phishing attacks can lead to identity theft, financial loss, and security breaches, compromising users' data and privacy.

Smishing and Vishing

Smishing is phishing via SMS, while vishing is phishing over the phone. Both aim to steal personal information through deceit.

Stay Safe Online

Use two-factor authentication, strong passwords, and update your software regularly to prevent phishing attacks and improve online security.

What is Pharming?

Pharming redirects you to fraudulent websites by manipulating your DNS or browser settings, allowing attackers to steal your personal data.
